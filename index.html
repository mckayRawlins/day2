Obi-Wan Kenobi

A legendary Jedi Master, Obi-Wan Kenobi was a noble man and gifted in the ways of the Force.

Accomplishments:

Defeating Darth Maul: Kenobi became a Jedi Knight in 32 BBY after defeating the Sith Lord Darth Maul during the Invasion of Naboo. This was the first time a Jedi had defeated a Sith Lord in a millennium.

Training Anakin Skywalker: Kenobi trained the prophesied Chosen One, Anakin Skywalker, who was destined to restore balance to the Force.

Serving as a general in the Clone Wars: Kenobi became a High Jedi General in the Grand Army of the Galactic Republic and led the Open Circle Fleet, the Third Systems Army, and the 7th Sky Corps.

Quotes:

"Hello there!" —to General Grievous, as he catches the leader of the droid army by surprise on the Separatist base in Utapau

"So uncivilized." —to a dead Grievous, after finally taking down the four-armed alien cyborg with a blaster instead of a lightsaber

"I have the high ground!" —to Anakin, as his apprentice attempts to strike him despite being on lower ground, a strategic disadvantage, during their epic duel on Mustafar

"You want to go home and rethink your life." —Obi-Wan performing a Jedi mind trick on a man who tries to sell him some death sticks as he was having a drink in a Coruscant nightclub
